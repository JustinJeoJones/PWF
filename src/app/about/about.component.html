My name is Paul Jones. I am a happily married father of 3. I have been a life long fan of professional wrestling and games. I started watching wrestling as a child in the 80's. It was how my father and I spent our time. My friends were into wrestling also, so we would make pen and paper wrestling games that we could play together. I enjoyed it so much I would continue making and perfecting wrestling games throughout my childhood. As I grew older and started a family I now spend time with my children watching wrestling and playing games.

I am also a fan of RPG's, CCG's and Deck building games. I have made a few of each over the years. In 1992 I made a Basket ball card game that would allow you to simulate an entire league, make trades, deal with injuries, manage rosters and a yearly draft. It was made out of index cards and I passed it on to my wife's younger brothers who played it for years after.

As an adult I have continued to make games and recently found ways to have them produced. I decided to give it a shot and share my latest creation with the world. I hope everyone enjoys it.
