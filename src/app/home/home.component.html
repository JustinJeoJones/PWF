Try to manage the chaos of Pro Wrestling Factions!

Have you ever wanted to become a pro-wrestling manager? Ever wanted to put your favorite wrestlers together in a faction? Here is your chance. In Pro Wrestling Factions (PWF) you can do just that. You can recruit wrestlers, put together legendary matches, or even try a new combination of wrestlers that have never been in a faction before. Make a run for the gold! Can you capture the Championships at the Pay Per View (PPV) or will you successfully defend the titles that you have earned along the way?
